The act of encoding information using a [[Cipher]] (i.e. a key and algorithm) so becomes unreadable by anyone without access to the cipher, for the purpose of protecting it or exchanging it securely.

[[Plaintext]] which is enciphered to become [[Ciphertext]] is said to be "encrypted".

Two primary classes of encryption systems are:
- [[Symmetric]], which use a single key
- [[Asymmetric]], which use key pairs

Synonymous with [[Encipherment]].
The reverse operation of [[Decryption]].
