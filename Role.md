A construct defined by a set of entitlements to perform a specific set of functions on a specific set of resources.

A role may be assigned to an [[Entity]].

Roles are used in applying Role-based Access Control or [[RBAC]].

Association of a role with an entity is commonly done by defining a [[Role Group]], and making the entity a member of the group.  This is generally done in [[Admin-time]], when managing the identity of that entity.  It may also be done [[Real-time]] through [[Just in-time]] request and approval of membership in that role, and usually expiry after a specified duration.

Other [[Real-time]] alternative to role-based [[Just in-time]] access are Attribute-based Access Control ([[ABAC]]) or Policy-based Access Control ([[PBAC]]).
