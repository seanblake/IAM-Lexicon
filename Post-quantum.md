Referring to the period after (hence, "post") [[Quantum computing]] methods capable of breaking traditional cryptographic schemes (AKA [[Classical cryptography]]) are in use.

[[Post-quantum Cryptography]] or [[Quantum resistant]] cryptographic schemes are designed to not be susceptible to attack using [[Quantum computing]].

[[Post-quantum Readiness]] is the act of preparing for a post-quantum world, where [[Classical cryptography]] is no longer safe for protecting information from adversaries with access to sufficient [[Quantum computing]] resources.
