Initialism of: [[Rivest-Shamir-Adelman]]

Refers to a [[Cryptosystem]] based on [[Asymmetric key Cryptography]] developed in 1977 at MIT by Ron Rivest, Adi Shamir and Leonard Adelman which takes its name from the first letters of the inventors' surnames.

***Disambiguation**:* may also refer to the trade name of a cyber-security company (RSA Security LLC) later founded by the three inventors of the cryptosystem.
