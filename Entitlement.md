Generally synonymous with [[Permission]].

A set of actions which an [[Entity]] may be permitted to perform on a [[Resource]] - generally a single instance of a resource, or a single type or other grouping of common resources.  E.g. read and modify a file; open doors to office space on specific floors of a building.

Generally, entitlements are associated with a [[Role]] - i.e. the holder of a role is entitled to perform the associated action(s) on the associated resource(s).
