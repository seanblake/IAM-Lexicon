In terms of [[Identity and Access Management]], admin-time refers to the period before an entity attempts to establish a [[Session]] to access a [[Resource]].

These are administrative activities (hence the name), and include things such as:
- Lifecycle of an entity's identity
- Managing an entity's entitlements

Admin-time activities are pre-requisite to the successful execution of [[Real-time]] activities - e.g. an entity must have an account established, be issued credentials, and granted entitlements (in admin-time) so they may authenticate themselves, and be authorized to access to resources to which they are entitled (in real-time).
