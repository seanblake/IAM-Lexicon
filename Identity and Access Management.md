Commonly abbreviated as [[IAM]] or [[ICAM]] (where the "C" stands for credential).

[[IAM]] is more commonly used in private industry, and [[ICAM]] is used more commonly in the public sector, at least in North America, specifically in the Canadian Federal Government by [[TBS]] and [[CCCS]], and in the US Federal Government by [[NIST]] and the [[FICAM]] program.

The practice of managing [[Identities]] and their associated [[Access]] to [[Resources]].

 The practice may be sub-divided into:
- [[Identity Management]]
- [[Credential Management]]
- [[Access Management]]
- [[Privileged Access Management]]
