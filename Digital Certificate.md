A data structure containing identity information of an entity, along with a [[Public Key]] corresponding to a [[Private key]] (held separately, and kept secret, by the entity.)

Digital certificates can be used for a number of purposes, including:
- [[Authentication]]
- [[Digital Signature]]
- [[Encryption]]

[[Public Key Infrastructure]] enables the use of digital certificates between multiple parties through common trust of entities which issue certificates, called [[Certification Authorities]].
