An [[Identity]] (more accurately, a sub-set of an identity) used within a specific [[Context]].

For example, a [[Person]] may have an "Employee" persona they act as when performing their job.  The set of attributes which describe them uniquely as an employee will not include attributes of that individual that are pertinent in other areas of their life, and which do not need to be shared with an employer to perform their duties.

An entity may have many personas but generally only acts as one persona at a time.
