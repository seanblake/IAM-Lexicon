In terms of Identity and Access Management, real-time refers to the period when:
- an entity attempts to establish a [[Session]] to access resources - i.e. authenticates itself, and requests authorization; and,
- during the session

Once a session is established, an entity generally does not have to re-authenticate themselves, but authorization decisions may be re-evaluated periodically or at every action.

Some real-time IAM functions are out-of-band of a session, such as logging and monitoring, and may be used to perform real-time or [[Post event-time]] functions.
