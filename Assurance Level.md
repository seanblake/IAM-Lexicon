Abbreviated as [AL]

Defined by [[NIST]] in [Special Publication 800-63](https://pages.nist.gov/800-63-4/sp800-63.html) for use by the US Federal Government, these are similar to [[Level of Assurance]] used by the Canadian Federal Government.

There are categories of assurance level (compared to two for [[LoA]]):
- Identity Assurance Level ([[IAL]])
- Authenticator Assurance Level ([[AAL]])
- Federation Assurance Level ([[FAL]])

Assurance Levels also have numerical and confidence levels as follows:

|  #  |   Level   |
| :-: | :-------: |
|  1  |   Basic   |
|  2  |   High    |
|  3  | Very High |
There is no direct mapping between AL and LoA.
