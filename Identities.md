The plural of [[Identity]].

Identities fall into two primary categories, and the management of each is largely the same in theory but in practice often requires distinct [[tools]].

The categories are often described using multiple different names, which are generally synonymous and may usually be used interchangeably.

Categories:
- [[Human]] or [[Person]]
- [[Non-human]] or [[Non-person]] or [[Machine]]

These categories are not necessarily comprehensive, but generally serve the purposes of managing [[Information Technology]].  E.g. an animal is not a person (i.e. [[Non-human]] or [[Non-person]]), but is also not a [[Machine]].  In general, this can be overlooked in theory, but may need considerations in practice.
